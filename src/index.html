Who is Merlin

Merlin is in charge of returning a portion of the revenue generated by Magic Internet Money to Spell stakers (sSpell & mSpell). The money used for the buyback includes fees, interest and 10% of liquidations. sSpell stakers receive Spell bought through TWAP transactions and mSpell receive MIM directly. Stakers from either contract recieve the same amount of value, but in their chosen token.

What is Spell

Spell is the token used for the Abracadabra defi protocol. Spell holders are granted voting rights and, if staked, a share of revenue.

Why Magic Internet Money

Magic Internet Money is a stablecoin which aims to be a cross-chain resource for all defi users. The $1 peg is maintained by arbitrage and is fullly collateralized by interest bearing assets making it highly decentralized. MIM adoption is the number one way to return value to Spell stakers.

Where can I stake

Staking is available on the Ethereum network using the Abracadabra site. It is also possible to trade Spell for sSpell using SushiSwap on the Avalanche network.
